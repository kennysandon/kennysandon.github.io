A collection of my work.

For the past 4 years design has been a critical part of my life. Whether that be with living spaces or technology. My design philosophy is to always to follow form with function. The product must be functional and the user must be able to extract its full potential.

Haptic Design.

By creating and rendering all my design into 3D space the client has a unique ability to connect deeper with my designs. It is important that the design is just right. By using VR it is possible to walk the client right into one of my designs.

Elite Living.

My speciality is personal living spaces. No matter the budget I believe it is possible to live in a hyper functional and elite space. With advanced design techniques it is possible to employ this philosophy into any space.
