My Work Experience

Crimson Midstream, LLC

Over the past summer I worked as a Business Development Intern for Crimson Midstream. Crimson is a midstream shipper of crude oil in California and the Gulf of Mexico. During my time with Crimson I worked as a data analyst and a Marketing Product Designer. As a data analyst I generated detailed shipping reports using raw meter data, historical data, and crude oil prices. As a marketing product designer I designed a “Technical Binder”. It is a complete and comprehensive overview of the company’s assets. The binder will be used as a marketing tool to educate clients on Crimson Midstreams shipping capabilities.

Allied Professionals Insurance Corporation

During the summers of 2016 and 2017 I spent my time as a Marketing Analyst / Software development intern for Allied Professionals Insurance Corporation (APIC). As a marketing analyst I analyzed competition and internal performance in the malpractice insurance market. I then developed and designed modern strategies to engage and attract new clientele. These included a fresh new website, and an Android app to collect client information at conventions and trade shows. As a software developer I designed 10 internal/external websites. With my team we also designed and developed an internal marketing material search engine/database. I also designed multiple online forums.

Smart Arrows

Smart Arrows is an ongoing personal project. I have recently picked up the sport of target archery and was looking for a way to improve my practice regime. In archery there are many factors that lead to a more accurate shot. Using Google Forms and Google Sheets, I developed a system to track shot progressions, yard performance, individual arrow performance, bow set up changes, and practice session statistics. I plan to turn this idea into a mobile based application.
