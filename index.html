Marketing the Future

Everyday we move towards a more connected world. My goal is to help nurture and grow the symbiotic relationship between humanity and the technology landscape. New products are released every day for new audience. It's my job to show people why these innovations are important and have a meaningful impact on their lives.

A Passion for Design

My experience in UI and UX and shown me how important design is. When a user interacts with one of my products I want them to already have an intimate relationship with it. This instinctual design is what I strive for in my products. Using software including, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Sketch, and MS Visual Studio, I craft a familiar feeling in my designs.

The Stronger the Team the Stronger the Product

Throughout my career I have worked on and lead many teams of varying sizes. These teams have produced many successful results. One thing that I have taken away from these experiences is the power of a strong team. Working together is vital in the workplace and is the basis of the human race.

Smart Arrows

Smart Arrows is an ongoing personal project. I have recently picked up the sport of target archery and was looking for a way to improve my practice regime. In archery there are many factors that lead to a more accurate shot. Using google forms and google sheets, I have developed a system to track shot progressions, yard performance, individual arrow performance, Bow set up changes, and practice session statistics. I plan to turn this idea into a mobile based application.
